Commands

Ability

You can either unlock/lock one specific ability or an entire group of abilities. For example "/ability give [abilityName] [playerName]" gives a single ability and "/ability unlock_group [abilityGroup] [playerName]" unlocks an entire group.


  • Give Singular Ability: "/ability give [abilityName] [playerName]"
  • Remove Singular Ability: "/ability remove [abilityName] [playerName]"
  • Give Ability Group: "/ability unlock_group [abilityGroup] [playerName]"
  • Remove Ability Group: "/ability lock_group [abilityGroup] [playerName]"
