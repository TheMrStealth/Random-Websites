Races

Humans

Humans have a normal amount of max mana and mana regeneration, however they level up much faster than the other 3 races do.

Elves

Elves have a much higher amount of max mana and regeneration than other races.

Hybrids

Hybrids are granted a second magic attribute (cannot be chosen) to utilize.

Witches

Items like a magic wand and a witch hat grant witches extra mana regeneration.
