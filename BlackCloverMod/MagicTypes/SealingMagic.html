Sealing Magic



Sealing Projectile

    • Self-Healing Sealing

  • Punches an entity, sealing him in place for a few seconds. Also empowers the punch.

    • Other Healing Sealing

    • Ultimate Sealing

  • Seals the enemy in a temporary seal
