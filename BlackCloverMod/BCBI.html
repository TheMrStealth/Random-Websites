Basic Info

Spell System

Once spawning in, use the spell given to you to kill mobs like bandits and wind monkey's to gain experience. Once you reach level 5, find a Grimoire Tower and talk to the Wizard there. He will give you your grimoire. After you receive your grimoire, you will gain a new spell every 5 levels, with some attributes having more spells than others.

Compatibility Issues

Please note that this mod has it's own curios api in it, so you do not need to add your modpack to equip the artifacts in this mod. There are currently no known compatibility issues, but if you find any, please let me know by dming me on discord (themrstealth).
