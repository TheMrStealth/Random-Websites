Quests


There are multiple quests you can get from the quest board in the Guild Master Base structure. Other than those, there are only 2 real quests so far.


Grimoire Quest

  • After spawning in, reach level 5 and talk to the magic man in a grimoire tower. After you talk to him, you should receive your grimoire.

    • Mana Reinforcement Quest

  • After receiving your grimoire, talk to the magic man a second time to receive the mana reinforcement quest. After receiving that quest, reach magic level 10 and come back. Talk to him at level 10 and you will receive the mana reinforcement ability.
